## ⚠️ You lose time in a bottle

In this update, you may lose all the collected _**Time** in a Bottle_. Please share your opinion about the fact that time no longer collects in a bottle [here](https://github.com/MagicJinn/Quantum-Things/issues/36).  
As a result, ![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/contenttweaker/bee_diversity__0.png "Bee Diversity Store") will not be operational for a while.
