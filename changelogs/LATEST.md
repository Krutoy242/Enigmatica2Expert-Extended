* <img src="https://i.imgur.com/YkGDlpp.png" align=right> [🖇](https://github.com/Krutoy242/Enigmatica2Expert-Extended/commit/1d1db756719cd815f6fbcc3c0c3df8941ba3aec3)✏️ Numishaul: making ![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/thermalexpansion/dynamo__5__9aae2683.png "Numismatic Dynamo (Basic)") great with just coins

  >
  > - Rebalances existing coins to account for their in-world presence, rarity and importance in E2E-Extended

  > - explains new coins and Lapidary augment nerf in English and Russian versions of questbook
  > - adds new burst seed that serves as a way to represent the sheer energy value of Infinity Ingot
