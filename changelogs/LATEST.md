## ‚ú® New Features

* <img src="https://i.imgur.com/yU5yzHl.png" align=right> [üñá](https://github.com/Krutoy242/Enigmatica2Expert-Extended/commit/8aa97427f178cc9c92207a1b9a38acbad84ba41d)‚≠êDraconic Evolution rework
  > The main goal of this rework is to make DE Cores, such as ![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/draconicevolution/chaotic_core__0.png "Chaotic Core") be available in midgame, rather to late game.
  > 
  > The reason for this is that Cores used to make spawners. Before change, there was no use for ![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/draconicevolution/draconic_spawner__0.png "Stabilized Mob Spawner"), since simple ones is too weak and advanced simply unavailable.
  > 
  > Now, players can make Stabilized spawners way earlier.
  > 
  > **Its possible to craft Chaotic Core without any progression in Tech or magic mods.**
  > 
  > Such drastic changes in Core recipes would make other DE progression too simple. Thats why Quest book and other DE recipes was changed too.
  > 
  > Most DE recipes was changed, but overall complexity and hardness of this recipes should be same.
  > 
  > Core's UU values now x100 times lower.
  > Also ![](https://github.com/Krutoy242/mc-icons/raw/master/i/draconicevolution/ender_energy_manipulator__0__6bebce59.png "Ender Energy Manipulator") way cheaper now for easier egg farming.
  > 
  > *If you find any too cheap / too grindy recipes after this patch - please note me.*
